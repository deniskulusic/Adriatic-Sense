What is included in the charter fee and what is APA?

The charter fee typically includes the yacht rental, crew wages, and insurance. The APA (Advance Provisioning Allowance) is an additional 20-30% of the charter fee used to cover operating expenses such as fuel, food, drinks, and port fees. Any unused APA is refunded at the end of the charter.

Can we customize our sailing itinerary?

Absolutely. While our skipper can suggest the best routes (like Hvar, Vis, or Kornati) based on weather and your preferences, the itinerary is fully flexible. We create a bespoke route tailored to your desires, whether you prefer secluded bays or vibrant towns.

What water toys and amenities are available on board?

Floramye is equipped with premium water toys including 2 Sea Scooters, SUP boards, electric scooters, a floating mat, and a dinghy for exploration. Onboard amenities feature a flybridge Jacuzzi, Starlink Wi-Fi, a cinema setup in the saloon, and a wine cellar.

Is the Floramye catamaran suitable for children?

Yes, the Alegria 67 is perfect for families. The catamaran design offers great stability and spacious decks. Our crew is experienced in hosting families, ensuring safety and fun for guests of all ages.

Can the chef cater to specific dietary requirements?

Certainly. Before your arrival, we will ask you to fill out a preference sheet. Our chef, Igor, creates personalized menus accommodating allergies, vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, or any other specific dietary needs without compromising on gourmet quality.
