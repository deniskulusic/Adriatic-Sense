Cookie Policy

INTRODUCTION

What are cookies?

Last modified: December 2025

This website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your user experience, analyze traffic, and enable personalized advertising on platforms such as Google, Facebook, Bing, and TikTok.

Cookies are small text files that a website saves on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit it. They allow the site to remember your settings and recognize your browser on your next visit.

CONSENT MANAGEMENT

How we manage your preferences

Our site uses the Complianz system to manage your consents. On your first visit, you have the option to accept all cookies, decline them, or adjust settings according to your preferences. You can change your choice at any time by clicking the "Manage Consent" icon in the corner of the screen.

Google Consent Mode v2: Our site fully supports Google Consent Mode v2. This means that if you decline marketing cookies, Google platforms receive only anonymous signals about the visit, without collecting personal data that could identify you.

CATEGORIES

Types of cookies we use

1. Functional Cookies (Essential):
Necessary for the basic functioning of the site (e.g., fleet search, security, language selection). These cookies cannot be turned off as the site would not work without them.

2. Analytical Cookies (Statistics):
We use Google Analytics to understand how you use the site. This data is anonymized and used exclusively to improve web performance.

3. Marketing Cookies (Tracking):
Since we actively advertise our services, we use cookies from the following platforms:
- Meta Pixel (Facebook/Instagram): For tracking ad performance and displaying relevant offers.
- Google Ads Tag: For remarketing and tracking conversions on Google search.
- Microsoft Advertising (Bing): For analyzing traffic from the Bing search engine.
- TikTok Pixel: For measuring the effectiveness of video campaigns.

4. AI Agent Cookies:
Our AI assistant may use cookies to remember your conversation history while you navigate the site.

USER CONTROL

How to control cookies?

Besides utilizing our consent banner, you can manage cookies through your web browser settings (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, etc.). Most browsers allow you to block all cookies or receive a notification before a cookie is stored on your device.
