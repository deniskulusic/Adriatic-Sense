Independency & Leisure

"Double Memory" is designed for those who value independence. Supported by solar panels, a generator, and a watermaker, you are free from the need to dock in crowded marinas.

Whether you are seeking adventure or pure relaxation, the yacht is equipped with air-conditioning and heating for all seasons. Located in Marina Kremik, one of the safest marinas in Croatia, it is your perfect gateway to the Kornati islands and Vis archipelago.

Is a skipper included in the charter fee?

For Double Memory, the skipper is obligatory and is paid as an extra (typically payable at the base). This ensures your safety and allows you to relax while a professional handles the navigation.

Where is the boat located?

Double Memory is based in Marina Kremik, Primošten. This is an ideal starting point, located halfway between the Kornati National Park and the islands of Hvar and Vis.

What is the cabin layout for guests?

Double Memory is configured to maximize guest comfort with 3 double cabins and 2 fully equipped bathrooms (toilets/showers) dedicated strictly to clients.

Note: There is a 4th separate cabin in the bow with its own toilet and two bunk beds, but this is strictly reserved for the crew (skipper and optional hostess) to ensure your privacy.

Does the boat have air conditioning?

Yes, Double Memory is equipped with full Air Conditioning and a Generator, ensuring comfort even during the warmest summer nights.

YOUR PROFESSIONAL CREW

Tea is a versatile professional who combines her experience as a skipper with a background in fine dining.
