INTRODUCTION

General Information

Adriatic Sense (Pajet d.o.o.) – Last modified: December 2025

At Adriatic Sense (Pajet d.o.o.), we value your privacy. This Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the EU AI Act.

CONTROLLER

Data Controller

Pajet d.o.o.
Address: Ulica Domovinskog rata 60, 21000 Split, Croatia
Email: info@adriaticsense.com

DATA COLLECTION

What data do we collect?

We collect only data necessary for providing charter services and website functionality:

Identification Data: Name, surname, date of birth, passport/ID number, citizenship (required for the e-Visitor system).
Evidence of Competence: Copies of nautical licenses and VHF certificates.
Contact Data: Email address, phone number.
Digital Behavior Data: IP address, browsing history of vessels (via cookies).
Communication Data: Content of inquiries via web forms or our AI agent.

AI TECHNOLOGY

Interactive Communication Systems (AI Agent)

We use an AI virtual assistant on our website to improve user experience.

Purpose: Providing information about the fleet, availability, and general charter conditions.
Processing: The AI agent processes data you voluntarily provide during the conversation. Conversations may be stored to analyze service quality and resolve your inquiries.
Note: Users are informed that they are communicating with an automated system (AI). Please do not share sensitive financial data (credit card numbers) via the AI agent.

TRACKING

Cookies and Advertising Platforms

Our site uses the Complianz system to manage consents. Your web behavior data is used for advertising on the following platforms, but exclusively with your consent:

- Meta (Facebook & Instagram): Conversion tracking and remarketing.
- Google (Ads & Analytics): Traffic analysis and targeted advertising (including Google Consent Mode v2).
- Microsoft (Bing Ads): Campaign performance tracking.
- TikTok: Analytics and video ad optimization.

You can withdraw your consent at any time via the cookie settings on our site.

LEGAL BASIS

Purpose and Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:
Contract Execution: Vessel charter and accompanying services.
Legal Obligation: Registration of guests in the e-Visitor system and Port Authorities.
Legitimate Interest: Vessel security and property protection.
Consent: Sending newsletters and marketing tracking.

SHARING

Who has access to your data?

Your data is shared only with necessary partners:
State Bodies: Ministry of Tourism and Port Authority (legal obligation).
Technical Service Providers: Hosting platforms, AI systems, and advertising networks (with anonymization where possible).
Accounting: For issuing invoices and tax records.

YOUR RIGHTS

What are your rights?

At any time, you have the right to:
- Access and correct your data.
- Deletion of data ("Right to be forgotten") – except for data we are legally legally obliged to keep.
- Restriction of processing and objection to marketing tracking.
- Data portability to another controller.

To exercise your rights, please contact us at: info@adriaticsense.com.

SECURITY

Data Security

All data is transmitted via a secure HTTPS connection (SSL certificate). We use modern protection methods to prevent unauthorized access to your data.
