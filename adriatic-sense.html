Independency & Leisure

"Adriatic Sense 1" is designed for those who value independence. Supported by solar panels, a generator, and a watermaker, you are free from the need to dock in crowded marinas.

Whether you are seeking adventure or pure relaxation, the yacht is equipped with air-conditioning and heating for all seasons. Located in Marina Kremik, Primošten, it is your perfect gateway to the Kornati islands and Vis archipelago.

Is a skipper included in the charter fee?

For Adriatic Sense 1, the skipper is obligatory (typically 1,750 € per week). This ensures a stress-free vacation where a professional manages the yacht's systems and navigation.

Where is the boat located?

Adriatic Sense 1 is based in Marina Kremik, Primošten. This strategic location offers excellent access to the Kornati National Park and the popular islands of Hvar, Brač, and Vis.

What is the cabin layout for guests?

Adriatic Sense 1 features 4 double guest cabins and 4 bathrooms.

There is an additional single cabin in the bow with its own toilet, which is designated for the skipper to ensure guest privacy. The saloon can also convert to offer 2 extra berths if needed.

Does the boat have air conditioning?

Yes, the yacht is fully equipped with Air Conditioning and a Generator (plus solar panels), ensuring comfort and power independence at anchor.
